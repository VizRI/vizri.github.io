2022Q1 - RI Political Expenditures

Date Tue 24 May 2022 Category blog Tags politics

Moving into 2022-Q1 with the election year in full swing, campaign accounts spent a total of ~$3,719,190.88 from 6,363 transactions an increase of +1,419,396 from 2021-Q4. With Seth Magaziner's pivot to the CD-2 race, he refunded a bunch (probably all?) of his donations, the most ever in a single quarter (2nd is 571,363 from Cicilline in 2010-Q1).

First looking at some of the totals, "Other" comes in with the most spent $1,312,135.97 (35%) this is largely dominated by the refunds from Magaziner's campaign account.
