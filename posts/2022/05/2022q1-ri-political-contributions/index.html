2022Q1 - RI Political Contributions

Date Mon 09 May 2022 Category blog Tags politics

Jumping into the campaign year with 2022-Q1 finance data campaigns raised a total of ~$4,809,597 from 12,593 transactions an increase of +1,150,846 from 2021-Q4. With Ashley Kalus's entrance to the race for governor she jumped into the second spot largely due to a $500,000 (and 20?) loan.
