2021Q4 - RI Political Expenditures

Date Mon 07 March 2022 Category blog Tags politics

Wrapping up the year with 2021-Q4 Campaign Expenditures, moving into the election year spending is heating up. Coming in at $2,273,368.76 spent in 5,066 transactions across 38 different states. These include aggregate expenditure, campaign expenditure, repayment of loans and refunds of contributions.

First looking at some of the totals, Consultant & Professional Services comes in with the most spent $555,142.04 (24%) and Political Donations with the most transactions with 1,126 for 20% of the total spent this quarter.
