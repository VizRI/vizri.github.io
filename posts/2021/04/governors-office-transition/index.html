Governor's Office Transition

Date Fri 09 April 2021 Category Blog Tags government / politics / payroll

To start I decided to take a look at some general information between the 2. This includes every employee on the payroll who wasn't terminated this quarter or the previous. While Mckee has 37 employees, a whopping 20 (54%) of them are missing any job title, I assume this is just a paperwork issue that will be resolved next quarter. It's also worth mentioning, while Mckee's highest earner is at 196,792 Gina's former chief of staff was close at 193,757 though wasn't included due to having a termination on 3/5/2021.

Okay, so with terminations and new hires out of the way... whats left? Thats right, the 15 holdovers from the Raimondo administration. Between last quarter and this quarter 15 employees remain on the Governor's Office payroll, 11 of these have had no change in their annual pay while the remaining 4 have received a raise since last quarter.

These raises consist of 2 3-4% raises, a 5% raise and a 23% raise. Once again it is noteworthy that, like new hires above, the transparent dataset lacks any sort of date with raises so these could have been decided under either administration.
