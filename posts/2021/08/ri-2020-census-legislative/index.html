2020 Census - Legislative Districts

Date Mon 16 August 2021 Category Blog Tags politics / census

Last week the first data drop for the 2020 census kicked off, this post takes a look at tables and maps showing the 610 (State Legislative District Upper) & 620 (State Legislative District Lower) sum levels, and the change between the 2012 ACS & 2020 Redistricting File (PL 94-171).

Changes: It was pointed out to me by Adam Myers that you can't compare the 2010 & 2020 PL 94-171 as they use different geographic boundaries because the districts are redrawn after the 2010 Decennial census. I opted to use the 2012 ACS Estimates and have corrected the tables below.
