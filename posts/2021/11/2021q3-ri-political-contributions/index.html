2021Q3 - RI Political Contributions

Date Tue 09 November 2021 Category blog Tags politics

The 2021-Q3 campaign finance data was released last week. Rhode Island politicians pulled in ~$2,346,312 dollars from 11,021 records, a bit of a drop from Q2.
