2021Q3 - RI Political Expenditures

Date Fri 19 November 2021 Category blog Tags politics

Taking a quick look at the 2021-Q3 Campaign Expenditures, this recent quarter saw more spending, but less transactions then the previous quarter coming in at $1,574,147.01 spent in 4,402 transactions across 34 different states. These include aggregate expenditure, campaign expenditure, repayment of loans and refunds of contributions.

First looking at some of the totals, while last quarter was dominated by political donations making up 35% of the total. This quarter saw a big jump in Consulting & Professional Services at $442,886.08 (28%) possibly a sign of candidates starting to plan their campaign strategies.
