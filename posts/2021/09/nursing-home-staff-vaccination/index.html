Nursing Home Staff Vaccination

Date Wed 01 September 2021 Category Blog Tags health / nursing / policy

With 1 month to go before the deadline for the vaccination mandate of health care workers, and 4 months until the mandatory staffing minimum. This takes a look at the most recent 8/26 release of nursing home data from CMS showing vaccinations numbers as of 8/15.

To start, 29 of the 75 nursing homes didn't update their numbers on 8/15/2021, so data shown is from the previous week 8/08/2021. The providers using data from the prior week are marked with a " * " at the end of their name. In addition, 1 home "HARRIS HEALTH CARE CENTER NORTH" didn't have any vaccination related numbers for staff members.

Currently only 1 nursing home has a 100% vaccination rate of it's staff "ELDERWOOD AT RIVERSIDE" and most homes sit around 70-80%. IF the current numbers remained unchanged Rhode Island is looking at eliminating ~25% of it nursing home staff which equates to roughly ~2,300 jobs.

It's important to note this data is from 8/15 the same week of the announcement and in the case of 29 homes, 8/8 prior to the announcement.
